Direction-of-arrival estimation in realistic radar

My M.S. thesis: comparing radar DoA algorithms (matched filter, MUSIC, compressed sensing) under multipath and noise — and using a denoising autoencoder to recover accuracy.

Designed a fully passive chipless RFID tag — no chip, no power — encoding data as impedance discontinuities, plus an Inverse SAR method to locate it. Best B.Tech. Project.
