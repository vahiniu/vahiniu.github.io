WanderYou

Mobile-first web app · AI-powered group trip planner · live

A mobile-first, AI-powered group trip planner that turns chaotic group-chat travel talk into a finished plan.

A walkthrough of WanderYou (sped up 3×)

We all know the pattern: someone floats a big trip idea in a group chat, a hundred messages pile up — dates, destinations, "who's in?" — and nothing ever gets decided. WanderYou fixes exactly that. You build a travel passport, gather your squad with a join code, and set your vibes, budget, and free dates. Then everyone swipes to vote on real destinations until a winner emerges — with a per-person vote breakdown, live flight prices from your own airport, and rich, cited detail on each place (weather, top things to do, what it's famous for).

Once you lock in dates, the app generates a day-by-day itinerary with dining picks, an AI packing checklist, and a local cheat sheet of phrases and etiquette. Friends plan together in real time — votes, chat, and the itinerary all sync live across phones. It's designed to degrade gracefully too: a 100+ destination offline fallback and a "fast mode" keep the flow from ever dead-ending.

Under the hood it's built with React 19, TypeScript, and Vite, styled with Tailwind, and powered by the Anthropic Claude API — using web search for grounded, sourced facts — with optional Supabase for real-time multiplayer.
