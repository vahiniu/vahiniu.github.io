🎰 Las Vegas & Page, Arizona

📍 Nevada & Arizona, USA ⏱️ 3 days

A quick desert getaway pairing the slot canyons and viewpoints around Page, Arizona with the shows, attractions, and dining of the Las Vegas Strip.

🗺️ Itinerary
Day 1

Drive to Page

Fly into Las Vegas and drive ~4.5 hours to Page. Stop at Horseshoe Bend, visit Lake Powell, and stargaze.

Day 2

Antelope Canyon & back to Vegas

Lower and Upper Antelope Canyon tours, then drive back to Las Vegas. Walk the Strip and catch a show.

Day 3

Vegas highlights & departure

Brunch, the Bellagio fountains, the Sphere, Omega Mart, and the Ethel M cactus garden before the evening flight home.

📌 Things to do
  • Horseshoe Bend (Page)
  • Lower and Upper Antelope Canyon guided tours (Page)
  • Toadstool Hoodoos & Glen Canyon Dam overlook
  • Lake Powell — kayaking
  • Omega Mart (Area15)
  • Bellagio fountains and conservatory
  • The Sphere
  • High Roller observation wheel at sunset
  • Penn & Teller and Absinthe shows
🍴 Where to eat
  • Truth & Tonic — vegan
  • Crossroads Kitchen — vegan
  • Saffron — vegan-friendly
  • Nacho Daddy — vegan options
🛏️ Where to stay
  • Page, Arizona — vacation rental
  • Las Vegas Strip hotel
💡 Good to know
  • Book Antelope Canyon tours in advance; lower and upper are separate tours.
  • Allow ~4.5 hours each way between Las Vegas and Page.
  • Be aware of Las Vegas resort fees on top of room rates.
