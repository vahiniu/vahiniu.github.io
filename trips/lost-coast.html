🥾 Lost Coast Trail

📍 King Range, Northern California, USA ⏱️ 4 days backpacking

A rugged 3-day backpacking trip along the Lost Coast Trail in the King Range, hiking roughly 24 miles point-to-point from Mattole Beach to Black Sands Beach, where tide timing is critical.

🗺️ Itinerary
Day 0

Drive to trailhead area

Roughly a 5-hour drive to lodging in Redway, near the trailhead.

Day 1

Shuttle & Mattole to Cooksie Creek

Early shuttle to Mattole Beach, then hike ~6.5 miles to Cooksie Creek, passing Punta Gorda Lighthouse.

Day 2

Cooksie Creek to Big Flat

Tide-sensitive ~9+ mile hike to Big Flat, up to 7 hours.

Day 3

Big Flat to Black Sands Beach

About an 8.5-mile hike out, also tide-sensitive.

📌 Things to do
  • Punta Gorda Lighthouse — historic lighthouse along the trail
  • Big Flat — scenic spot to relax and explore
  • Beach and bluff hiking along the King Range coastline
🛏️ Where to stay
  • Motel in Redway, CA — pre-hike base
  • Backcountry camping at Cooksie Creek and Big Flat
💡 Good to know
  • Carefully plan around tides — some sections are impassable at high tide (use NOAA tide predictions).
  • A bear canister is required; rent one ahead.
  • Use a point-to-point shuttle service — book in advance.
  • Bring reliable water filtration; treat clothing with permethrin for ticks.
  • Campfires are banned — bring a stove.
