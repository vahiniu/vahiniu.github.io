🌋 Lassen Volcanic National Park

📍 California, USA ⏱️ 3 days, camping

A summer car-camping weekend at Lassen Volcanic National Park, basing at Summit Lake and mixing easy lakeside loops with a strenuous summit of Lassen Peak.

🗺️ Itinerary
Day 1

Arrival & set up camp

Arrive in the evening and set up camp; an easy hike like the Manzanita Lake loop or Echo Lake trail.

Day 2

Full day of hiking

Summit Lassen Peak in the morning (~5 miles, 2,000 ft), then Bumpass Hell in the evening; optional Kings Creek Falls.

Day 3

Sunrise & departure

Optional sunrise hike, break camp, and leave by early afternoon.

📌 Things to do
  • Lassen Peak — strenuous summit hike (~5 miles, ~2,000 ft)
  • Bumpass Hell — hydrothermal basin trail (~2.7 miles)
  • Manzanita Lake loop — easy 2-mile sightseeing loop
  • Echo Lake trail — ~5 miles, trailhead at the campground
  • Kings Creek Falls / Bench & Sifford Lakes loop (~5 miles)
  • Kohm Yah-mah-nee Visitor Center
🛏️ Where to stay
  • Summit Lake South Campground
💡 Good to know
  • Campfires are not allowed at the campsite — bring a camp stove and gas.
  • Mosquitoes are heavy in June/July as snow melts; bring strong bug spray.
  • No showers at the campground; pack wilderness wipes.
  • Store all food properly per bear-safety guidance.
