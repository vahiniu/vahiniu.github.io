🍷 Sonoma & Ukiah Wine Country

📍 Sonoma & Mendocino County, California ⏱️ 2 days

A cozy rainy-weekend road trip from San Francisco through the Sonoma redwoods and wineries up to Ukiah, mixing misty nature stops, wine tasting, and vegetarian dining.

🗺️ Itinerary
Day 1

Redwoods, wine & Ukiah

Drive north on US-101. Armstrong Redwoods State Natural Reserve, lunch in Healdsburg, wine tasting at Ridge Vineyards, then dinner in Ukiah.

Day 2

Ukiah sights & scenic return

Breakfast and yoga, then the City of Ten Thousand Buddhas before the scenic drive back to San Francisco.

📌 Things to do
  • Armstrong Redwoods State Natural Reserve — Pioneer Nature Trail
  • Ridge Vineyards — Zinfandel and Cabernet tasting
  • Mill Creek Vineyards — BYO-picnic tasting
  • City of Ten Thousand Buddhas — Buddha Hall and gardens
  • Optional stroll near Lake Mendocino
🍴 Where to eat
  • Cultivo (Ukiah) — vegetarian; tacos, enchiladas, veggie bowls
  • Jyun Kang Vegetarian Restaurant — dumplings and noodle soups
  • Healdsburg Shed — farm-to-table light lunches
  • Dry Creek General Store — deli sandwiches
🛏️ Where to stay
  • Ukiah — vacation rental
💡 Good to know
  • A great rainy-weekend option — tasting rooms and the temple work indoors.
  • Ukiah is about 40 minutes north of the Healdsburg wineries.
