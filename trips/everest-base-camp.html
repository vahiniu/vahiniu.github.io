🏔️ Everest Base Camp Trek

📍 Khumbu Region, Nepal ⏱️ 12 days

A classic 12-day trek through Nepal's Khumbu region to Everest Base Camp (5,364m) and Kala Patthar (5,550m), with a scenic helicopter return. The route passes Sherpa villages, monasteries, and dramatic Himalayan peaks.

🗺️ Itinerary
Day 1

Arrive in Kathmandu (1,345m)

Transfer to hotel, trek briefing, and final gear shopping in the city.

Day 2

Fly to Lukla (2,840m) & trek to Phakding

Scenic flight to Lukla, then a gradual descent along the Dudh Koshi River, crossing suspension bridges.

Day 3

Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

Through pine forest past Monjo (Sagarmatha NP entrance) and Jorsale, with a steep climb to Namche and first Everest views.

Day 4

Acclimatization: Everest View Hotel

Hike to the Everest View Hotel for panoramas of Ama Dablam, Thamserku, and Everest, plus the Everest Museum.

Day 5

Trek to Deboche (3,860m)

Climb to Tengboche to visit its iconic monastery, then continue to peaceful Deboche.

Day 6

Trek to Dingboche (4,430m)

Pass through Pangboche into high-altitude terrain with stone-walled fields.

Day 7

Acclimatization hike to Nangkartshang (5,066m)

A climb to a ridge with views of Makalu, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam, then back to Dingboche.

Day 8

Trek to Lobuche (4,910m)

Ascend the lateral moraine of the Khumbu Glacier with views of the Khumbu Icefall.

Day 9

Everest Base Camp (5,364m)

Trek across glacial moraine to Base Camp, then return to Gorakshep (5,164m) for the night.

Day 10

Kala Patthar (5,550m) & descent

Pre-dawn climb to Kala Patthar for sunrise over Everest, Lhotse, and Nuptse, then descend to Pheriche.

Day 11

Return to Kathmandu

Helicopter return option and Lukla–Kathmandu flight.

Day 12

Departure

Depart Kathmandu.

📌 Things to do
  • Everest Base Camp (5,364m) — the trek's main destination
  • Kala Patthar (5,550m) — best sunrise viewpoint over Everest
  • Tengboche Monastery — the region's most important religious site
  • Everest View Hotel — ridge-top panoramas during acclimatization
  • Namche Bazaar — vibrant Sherpa hub town
  • Sagarmatha National Park — entered at Monjo
🛏️ Where to stay
  • Hotel in Kathmandu (start and end of trip)
  • Teahouses/lodges along the trail (Phakding, Namche, Deboche, Dingboche, Lobuche, Gorakshep, Pheriche)
💡 Good to know
  • Build in acclimatization days (Namche and Dingboche) to reduce altitude-sickness risk.
  • Carry USD for the helicopter return and rupees for the road.
  • Down jackets and sleeping bags can be rented in Kathmandu or Namche.
  • Get a local SIM and insurance that covers high-altitude trekking and evacuation.
  • Pack layers: thermals, merino base layers, fleece, and a waterproof shell.
