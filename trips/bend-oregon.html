🌲 Bend, Oregon

📍 Bend, Oregon, USA ⏱️ 3 days

A summer long-weekend in Bend mixing river floats and lava-tube caves with downtown coffee, food carts, and a lively cocktail-bar scene.

🗺️ Itinerary
Day 1

Arrival & downtown

Explore downtown Bend and the Old Mill District, then an evening Moonlight Canoe Tour with Wanderlust Tours.

Day 2

Newberry & river float

Newberry National Volcanic Monument (Lava River Cave, Big Obsidian Flow, Paulina Peak), then a Deschutes River float from Riverbend Park to Drake Park.

Day 3

Sauna & falls

A sauna session, brunch downtown, then a hike near Tumalo Falls or Smith Rock before flying out of Redmond.

📌 Things to do
  • Moonlight Canoe Tour with Wanderlust Tours
  • Deschutes River float — Riverbend Park to Drake Park
  • Newberry National Volcanic Monument — Lava River Cave (headlamp required), Big Obsidian Flow, Paulina Peak
  • Tumalo Falls hikes — 2 to 6.9-mile options
  • Smith Rock State Park — Misery Ridge Trail (~30 min from Bend)
  • Soaking pool at McMenamins Old St. Francis School
  • Old Mill District and downtown Bend
🍴 Where to eat
  • Backporch Coffee Roasters — coffee
  • Thump Coffee — coffee
  • The Sparrow Bakery — the famous Ocean Roll
  • McKay Cottage Restaurant — popular brunch
  • Wild Rose Northern Thai Eats — dinner
  • Bonta Gelato (Downtown Bend) — gelato
  • Dogwood at The Pine Shed — craft cocktails
  • San Simón — trendy cocktail bar
🛏️ Where to stay
  • Bend (NW / downtown-adjacent) — central to downtown, the Old Mill District, and the river
💡 Good to know
  • Lava River Cave requires a vehicle reservation and a headlamp.
  • Reserve river-float tube rentals up to 24 hrs ahead.
  • Fly in/out of Redmond (RDM), about 30 min from Bend.
  • Newberry parking is covered by a national park pass.
